Risk Management

Our Philosophy

We believe risk management is the foundation of successful trading. Our rules aren't designed to catch you out — they're designed to protect both you and us.

The 5% daily loss limit and 10% maximum drawdown exist because professional fund managers operate with similar constraints. If you can trade profitably within these limits, you can trade profitably at any scale.
