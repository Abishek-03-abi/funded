How It Works

Your Path to a Funded Account

From challenge purchase to your first payout — here's exactly how the process works.

1

Buy Challenge

Choose your preferred account size ($10K – $200K) and pay the one-time challenge fee. You'll receive login credentials to your evaluation account immediately.

2

Pass Evaluation

Trade the evaluation account and hit the 8% profit target while staying within the daily loss limit (5%) and maximum drawdown (10%). No time limit on most challenges.

3

Get Funded

Once you pass, we'll set up your funded account within 24 hours. You'll get the same account size with real capital behind it. Start trading immediately.

4

Withdraw Profits

Keep 80% of every dollar you make. First payout after 14 days, then weekly withdrawals via crypto, bank transfer, or other supported methods.

Evaluation Details

What Happens at Each Stage

📋 Challenge Phase

  • • Profit target: 8% of account balance
  • • Daily loss limit: 5% of starting equity
  • • Maximum drawdown: 10% of initial balance
  • • Minimum trading days: 5
  • • No maximum time limit
  • • All markets available: Forex, Gold, Indices, Crypto

🏆 Funded Phase

  • • Same account size as evaluation
  • • Same risk rules apply
  • • 80% profit split (you keep 80%)
  • • First payout: 14 days after start
  • • Subsequent payouts: weekly
  • • Scale up to $200K with consistent performance

What If I Fail?

No worries — you can reset your challenge at a discounted rate or purchase a new one. Learning from losses is part of the journey. Our reset fee is only 20% of the original challenge price.
