Refund Eligibility

You may request a full refund of your challenge fee if no trades have been placed on your evaluation account within 14 days of purchase.

Non-Refundable Conditions

  • Once any trade has been placed on the evaluation account
  • If the account has been breached (daily loss or max drawdown)
  • If more than 14 days have passed since purchase (regardless of activity)
  • Reset fees are non-refundable

How to Request a Refund

Contact support@fundededge.com with your account ID and reason for refund. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

Challenge Fee After Passing

The challenge fee is not refunded upon passing the evaluation. The fee covers the cost of the evaluation platform and account setup.
