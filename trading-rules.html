Trading Rules

Know the Rules

Clear, transparent trading rules. No hidden clauses, no surprises.

📊 Risk Limits

Rule Limit Description
Daily Loss Limit 5% Maximum loss in a single day based on starting equity.
Maximum Drawdown 10% Max drawdown from initial balance. Below this = breach.
Profit Target 8% Evaluation phase target. Not applicable when funded.
Min Trading Days 5 days Must trade on at least 5 calendar days.
Max Leverage 1:100 Forex 1:100, Indices 1:50, Crypto 1:10.

🚫 Prohibited Strategies

These will result in account termination and profit forfeiture.

  • Arbitrage Trading — Exploiting price differences between brokers.
  • High-Frequency Trading — Hundreds of orders per second.
  • Tick Scalping — Opening/closing within 1-2 ticks.
  • Latency Abuse — Exploiting feed or execution delays.
  • Copy Trading Between Accounts — Duplicating trades across FundedEdge accounts.
  • Account Sharing — Only the registered holder may trade.
  • Martingale Without Stop Loss — Position stacking without risk management.

✅ Allowed Strategies

  • News Trading — Trade during high-impact news freely.
  • Expert Advisors (EAs) — Bots allowed if not HFT or latency-based.
  • Swing Trading — Overnight and weekend holds permitted.
  • Scalping — Allowed (min 15-second hold time).
  • Hedging — Opposing positions on same instrument OK.
  • All Markets — Forex, Gold, Indices, Crypto.

⚠️ Rule Violations

Soft Breach

Daily loss exceeded — all trades closed, no trading that day.

Hard Breach

Max drawdown exceeded — account terminated, fee forfeited.

Strategy Violation

Prohibited strategy used — termination and potential ban.
